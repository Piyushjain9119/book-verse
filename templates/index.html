J. K. Rowling

J.K. Rowling is a British author best known for creating the Harry Potter series, a global literary phenomenon.Her work has influenced an entire generation, spawning a major film series, theme parks, and a vast cultural legacy.

Barbara Kingsolver

Barbara Kingsolver is an American author and biologist known for her richly detailed novels that often explore themes of social justice and the environment.

J.R.R. TOLKIEN

J.R.R. Tolkien was an English writer and philologist renowned for creating the epic fantasy novels "The Hobbit" and "The Lord of the Rings" series.

Neil Gaiman

Neil Gaiman is a British author celebrated for his imaginative works in fantasy and horror, including "American Gods" and "The Sandman" series.

Janet Evanovich

Janet Evanovich is an American author best known for her popular mystery series featuring the quirky bounty hunter Stephanie Plum.
